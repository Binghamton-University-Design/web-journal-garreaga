STORYBOARD OVERVIEW

Mystery Flesh Pit National Park has a lot of information about it out there- that's why I want to generate an interactive user expirience to explore it. Using both a linenar narrative and free form exploratory set of information, visitors to the site can expirience what it was like to be both a park-goer during the 2007 disaster, and park staff looking at their own pace through whatever piques their interest.
